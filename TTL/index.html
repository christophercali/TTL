Welcome to the Twisted Tea-Lympics!

We're turning a small Brooklyn backyard into a high-stakes arena where teams will compete in a tournament of drinking games. Get ready for a twisted time with Twisted Tea!

Games

3-2-1 Tea Pong

A traditional game of 2v2 beer pong, but with a twist! Set up with only 6 cups instead of 10.

Grab Cup (NO SLAP!)

Traditional rules for slap cup, but instead of slapping, grab the cup when you bounce on someone and place it in your team's pile. Each cup grabbed earns your team 1 point.

Flip Cup

A full team game for all 4 members. You know the rules. Best of 3.

Chandelier

Bounce the ball and flip. Each player has 3 lives. Don't squander them!

FINAL GAME: SHOTEA

To be explained at the sparkler lighting ceremony.
